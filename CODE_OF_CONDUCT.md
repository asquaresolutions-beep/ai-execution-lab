# Code of Conduct

**Copyright © 2026 A Square Solutions. All Rights Reserved.**

A Square Solutions is committed to a respectful, harassment-free environment for
everyone who interacts with TrustSeal — users, customers, researchers, and
contributors.

## Our standards

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful, professional, and considerate.
- Assume good faith; give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful conduct of any kind.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or deliberate intimidation.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Abuse of the TrustSeal service, including fraudulent verification claims or
  badge misuse (see the [Trademark Policy](TRADEMARKS.md)).

## Scope

This applies to all TrustSeal spaces and to interactions with the A Square
Solutions team in connection with TrustSeal.

## Reporting & enforcement

Report violations to **contact@asquaresolution.com**. Reports are handled
confidentially. A Square Solutions may warn, suspend, or ban offenders and may
revoke access to the TrustSeal service for serious or repeated violations.

**Official contact:** contact@asquaresolution.com
