## Allowed uses

- Factual, nominative reference to TrustSeal (e.g., "We verify our domain with TrustSeal").
- Displaying the official "Verified by TrustSeal" badge **only** on a domain you
  have verified through TrustSeal, served via the official badge loader, while the
  verification remains active.
- Linking to `https://trustseal.asquaresolution.com` and to your public seal page.

## Forbidden uses

- Using the Marks in a way that implies endorsement, partnership, or affiliation
  that does not exist.
- Brand impersonation — registering domains, accounts, apps, or social handles that
  imitate TrustSeal or A Square Solutions.
- Altering, recoloring, redrawing, or recombining the logo or trust marks.
- Reproducing the verification graphics, trust-score indicators, or badge designs
  outside the official badge system.
- Displaying a "Verified" badge for a domain that is not actually verified, or
  retaining a badge after verification has expired or been revoked
  ("**fraudulent verification claims**" — see also abuse reporting below).
- Using the Marks as part of your own product, service, or company name.

## Logo & badge restrictions

- Use the official badge embed only; do not self-host, screenshot, or hardcode a
  static copy. The official badge checks live status and is origin-bound.
- Do not modify badge markup to suppress downgrade/expiry behavior.

## Brand impersonation & badge misuse

Impersonation and fraudulent verification claims undermine the trust the Marks
represent and may constitute trademark infringement and consumer fraud. We pursue
takedowns and other remedies for such misuse.
