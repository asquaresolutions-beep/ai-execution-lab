URL-level audit of the live WordPress site (enumerated from the sitemaps: 12 pages + **745 unique blog posts**). **Method note:** this is a *slug/URL-level* audit (intent, overlap, cannibalization, clustering, bloat) — body-level depth/E-E-A-T is inferred from pattern, not read for all 745. Pair with the GSC "Crawled – not indexed" export to confirm which Tier-C URLs Google is already ignoring (almost certainly most of the news/science filler).

## Executive finding
The blog reads as a **generic AI/tech news site**, not an AI-SEO/automation/scam-intelligence authority. Of 745 posts, only **~12–15 are strategically on-topic.** The rest dilute topical authority and create index bloat:
- **~94** science/space/physics posts (quantum, telescopes, batteries, robotics, whales…)
- **~29** geopolitics/world-news (oil, ceasefires, shutdowns, inflation…)
- **~21** regional/Dubai "top digital marketing companies in X" listicles (duplicate intent, weak for a UK/global AI agency)
- the bulk of **~100** generic "AI news" posts
**Implication:** trimming/noindexing this dilution is likely the single biggest topical-authority lever — it tells search + AI engines what this site is actually about.

1. **Regional/Dubai digital-marketing listicles → one hub.** Build a single "Digital marketing companies & trends (global)" resource; **301 the rest into it**, keep the **UK** one and link it to `/digital-marketing-uk/`. Or noindex all but UK if they have no links/traffic.

3. **AI-news roundups → keep 1–2 evergreen explainers, noindex/prune the dated rest.** Don't try to maintain a news desk.
4. **Science/space/geopolitics → bulk noindex** (or move off-domain / delete). They cannot be made topically relevant.
