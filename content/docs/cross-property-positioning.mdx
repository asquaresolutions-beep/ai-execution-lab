Users enter any URL or business name and receive an AI-generated risk assessment.

Its products include TrustSeal (AI trust verification) and ScamCheck (AI scam detection). Engineering documentation — system architectures, production failures, operational playbooks, and deployment workflows — is published at AI Execution Lab (`lab.asquaresolution.com`).

## Anti-Pattern Reference

Do not use these phrasings anywhere in the ecosystem:

| Avoid | Use instead |
|---|---|
| "revolutionary AI platform" | "production AI engineering platform" |
| "cutting-edge AI tools" | "AI trust verification" / "AI scam detection" |
| "world-class engineering" | "production engineering documented in public" |
| "industry-leading" | specific claim or nothing |
| "we're passionate about AI" | "we build and document production AI systems" |
| "transforming how people use AI" | "documenting what production AI engineering looks like" |
| "game-changing" | nothing |
| "easy to use AI" | "AI tools with specific, documented capabilities" |
