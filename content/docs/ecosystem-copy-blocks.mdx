## Copy Anti-Patterns to Avoid

These are patterns that break the premium, non-marketing aesthetic:

**Do not use:**
- "Check out our amazing AI Lab!"
- "We're excited to share..."
- "Discover the power of..."
- "Our cutting-edge tools..."
- Badge/award language ("trusted by 1000+ users")
- Urgency copy ("Sign up now before...")
- Generic agency copy ("We help businesses grow")

**Use instead:**
- Declarative statements about what things actually do
- Specific facts over vague claims
- "See" / "Read" / "Documented at" over "Discover" / "Explore" / "Check out"
- Product names and URLs over vague "our tools" references

The tone across all four properties should read as an operator writing for other operators — not as marketing copy written for a general audience.
